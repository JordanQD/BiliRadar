# BiliRadar Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

BiliRadar is a Windows desktop application for viewing Bilibili updates, history, watch-later items, and related notifications. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

## Data We Do Not Collect

BiliRadar does not operate its own account system and does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal information with the developer.

BiliRadar does not intentionally collect analytics, advertising identifiers, crash reports, browsing history, or usage telemetry for the developer.

## Data Used by the App

When you sign in to Bilibili in BiliRadar, the app may process Bilibili login cookies, access tokens, account profile responses, followed creator information, video update information, live room information, history records, watch-later records, and notification state. This data is used only to provide the app features you request.

Network requests made by BiliRadar are sent directly from your device to Bilibili services, Bilibili web pages, or links you choose to open. BiliRadar does not proxy this data through a developer-operated server.

## Local Storage

BiliRadar stores app settings and notification state on your device using Windows local application storage.

If you sign in, BiliRadar stores the login data needed to keep you signed in on your device. You can clear the saved login state from inside the app.

If you export app settings, the exported file may include your saved login cookie and other configuration data. Treat exported configuration files as private and do not share them with others unless you have reviewed their contents.

## Third-Party Services

BiliRadar is not affiliated with or endorsed by Bilibili. When the app connects to Bilibili, your use of Bilibili services is also subject to Bilibili's own terms and privacy policy.

If you open external links from the app, those sites may collect data according to their own policies.

## Data Sharing

BiliRadar does not send your personal data to the developer. The app only sends data to third-party services when necessary for features you use, such as signing in to Bilibili, loading Bilibili content, modifying watch-later items, following or unfollowing creators, or opening external links.

## Children's Privacy

BiliRadar is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated when BiliRadar's features or data handling change. The updated policy will be published with a new "Last updated" date.
